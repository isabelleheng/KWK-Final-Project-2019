Mission Statement

We are a group of girls, ages 14-17 years-old, making this website to create a comfortable and safe community for people of all backgrounds to share their story. No matter the race, gender, or sexuality, we want you to all come together on this forum. This is an absolute NO JUDGEMENT zone. Everyone here is treated as equals and no one is left out. We want you all to enjoy this website and shine your light on each other.

“Tell me your story. I want to hear your voice and I want to hear your conviction. No matter who you are, where you’re from, your skin color, your gender identity, just speak yourself. Find your name find your voice by speaking yourself.”

-Kim Namjoon
